company Profile

A Creative Architecture Agency For Your Dream Home

Our Founder Late Mr. Augustine John was born on 1952 at Ernakulum.

After 12 years of his service in construction field, he found THE JOHN’S COMPANY on 1989.

He understood his client’s needs and worked with them in a friendly manner to build their dream home.

He always used to say that “when we are building our client’s dream home, we must built it with quality and there should not be any compromise on it” .

Even now we are following his fundamental ideology.

Johns belongs to the largest and most reliable home interior design And Architecture Company in Kochi with more than 500 employees.

Mission and Vision

What Drives Us

Our mission is to create inspiring and sustainable spaces that enhance life quality. We are on our way to establish new benchmarks in creating home development by using advanced technologies, adopting eco-friendly practices, and employing avant-garde design concepts. At EV John's Concepts, we believe that we do not only build structures but also create communities that flourish in harmony with nature.

Where We're Headed

To be a global leader in the architecture and construction industry, recognized for our pioneering spirit, unwavering integrity, and commitment to delivering projects that surpass expectations. We envision a future where our developments contribute significantly to the advancement of smart cities and sustainable urban living.
