The Legacy of Our Founder:
Late Mr. Augustine John

Our story begins with the visionary Late Mr. Augustine John, born in 1952 in Ernakulam, Kerala. After 12 years of dedication in the construction industry, he founded The John’s Company in 1989, driven by a passion for creating homes that reflected his clients' dreams.

Mr. Augustine believed in more than just building structures—he focused on forging personal connections with clients and delivering uncompromising quality. His famous words, "When building a client’s dream home, quality must never be compromised," remain the cornerstone of our approach today.

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted leader in home interior design and architecture in Kochi, The John’s Company has grown to over 500 employees. Rooted in Mr. Augustine's values, we continue to create homes that embody his vision of quality, care, and integrity.

Continuing the Legacy: Mr. Shan John’s Leadership

Under the leadership of Mr. Shan John, son of the late Mr. Augustine John, The John’s Company continues to thrive while embracing its core values of quality, integrity, and client-focused service.

Shan John blends a modern vision with the timeless principles his father instilled, expanding the company's portfolio by integrating innovation with tradition. His leadership ensures the company remains a trusted name while pioneering new trends and technologies in the industry.

With a team of over 500 professionals, Shan John upholds the legacy of creating homes that combine aesthetics, functionality, and the uncompromising quality that defines The John’s Company.
